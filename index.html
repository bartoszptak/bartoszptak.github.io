About Me

I am associated with Poznan University of Technology (PUT) since 2021, having earned a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and subsequently completing a Master's Degree in Robotics and Autonomous Systems with honors in July 2021.

Presently, I am actively pursuing a Ph.D., focusing my research on the nuanced field of small object detection from Unmanned Aerial Robots. My approach integrates both deep learning and classical methods to address the complexities of this task.

In addition to my academic endeavors, I contribute to various robotics projects encompassing ground, agricultural, and space-oriented applications. My involvement is particularly marked in scenarios where computer vision converges with robotics, especially on Edge AI devices.
